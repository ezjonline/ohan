About Us

Bridging the Gap in Oral Healthcare

OHAN is an independent informational dental care directory dedicated to making dental care information more accessible and easier to understand for everyone.

Our goal is to help individuals and families locate dental clinics and better understand available oral health resources using publicly available information sources.

Access for Everyone

Everyone deserves to understand their oral health options. However, navigating the system often involves barriers like complex terminology and difficult-to-locate provider data.

OHAN bridges this gap by aggregating information from public databases and partner sources, providing a simplified way to explore dental care resources in your community.

Who is OHAN for?

  • Individuals without traditional dental insurance
  • Families seeking information on low-cost clinic options
  • Anyone looking to better understand oral health resources

What OHAN Does

  • Locates local dental clinics using public provider data.
  • Provides clarity on common oral health terms and resources.
  • Explains different types of dental providers and payment options.
  • Helps you prepare for your next dental visit with reliable information.

What OHAN Does NOT Do

  • No Medical Diagnosis: We cannot diagnose or treat any dental conditions.
  • No Clinical Care: We are an information directory, not a healthcare provider.
  • No Medical Advice: Our content is for informational purposes and does not replace professional advice.
  • Not for Emergencies: If you are experiencing a dental emergency, seek immediate professional care or call 911.

Built on Transparency

We believe that access to healthcare information should be clear and unbiased. OHAN is committed to transparency in how we surface information, ensuring that individuals can explore their options with confidence and independence.

Important Notice: OHAN Health is for informational purposes only. Always consult a licensed professional for diagnosis and treatment.
