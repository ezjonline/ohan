Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: March 2026

OHAN Health is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

1. Conformance Standards

OHAN Health is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with a wide array of disabilities.

2. Technical Specifications

Accessibility of OHAN Health relies on the following technologies to work with the particular combination of web browser and any assistive technologies or plugins installed on your computer:

  • HTML
  • WAI-ARIA
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

3. Barrier Reporting & Feedback

Despite our best efforts to ensure accessibility of OHAN Health, there may be some limitations. We welcome your feedback. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers on our Service by contacting us via email at accessibility@ohanhealth.com or through our Contact page.

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within 5 business days.
