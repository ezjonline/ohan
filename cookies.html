Cookie Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

OHAN Health uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our website.

1. Cookie Usage & Categorization

OHAN Health uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. We categorize these cookies as follows:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the core functionality of the site, such as navigation and accessing secure areas.
  • Preference Cookies: Allow the site to remember choices you make (like your preferred clinic search filters).
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the Service by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

2. Third-Party Cookies

We utilize third-party services like Google Analytics and Hotjar to analyze traffic and user behavior. These providers may set their own cookies on your browser to collect information about your online activities across different websites.

3. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, some portions of our Service may not function as intended.

To find out more about cookies, including how to see what cookies have been set and how to manage and delete them, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
